Red Fox

Red foxes can communicate using 28 different sounds with each other!

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes Vulpes
  • Average Length: 45-90cm
  • Average Lifespan: 3-4yrs
  • Habitat: Northern Hemisphere & Australia

Red Foxes have small builds but a strong one, with a thick fur coat, bushy tail, and excellent hearing. They have strong family dynamics and often choose to remain with one mate for life.
